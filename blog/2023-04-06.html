How to say my name

2023-04-06

This morning I was listening to a great recorded talk by Daniel Conley on the Emergence of Diatoms, courtesy of the 'Diatom Web Academy'. At some point of the talk, he happened to mention my name – I know: humble brag much :) – and, as every single person I ever met in my life, he mispronounced my name (no judgement here, as I said, it happens to literally everyone).

As it is the third time someone does this, this week alone, I thought I'll help people with the correct pronunciation. This is how one should pronounce my name: /ʒoan ʁə.no.di/
